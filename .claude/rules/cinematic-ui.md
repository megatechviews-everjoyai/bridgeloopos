# Cinematic & Artisan UI Standards
Scope: **/*.tsx, **/*.ts, **/*.css

## Objective
Merge the "AI Engineering" aesthetic of EverjoyAI with the "Artisan Luxury" of Lave Gallery.

## Styling Rules
- **Backgrounds:** Use deep black (#000000) with subtle mesh gradients or galaxy-inspired overlays.
- **Components:** Apply 'Glassmorphism'—background: rgba(255,255,255,0.03), backdrop-filter: blur(12px), border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.1).
- **Cards:** Use rounded corners (16px+) and subtle outer glows on hover rather than heavy shadows.
- **Text:** Headers must be uppercase with 0.1em letter-spacing. Body text should be DimGray (#696969) or Off-White (#F5F5F5) for readability.

## Tone & Vibe
- **Terminology:** Use terms like "Engineering," "Everjoy," "Vibe Check," and "Resonance."
- **Interaction:** Animations should be "liquid" and smooth (use Framer Motion for transitions).
